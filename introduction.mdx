## What Profound does

Profound continuously queries AI assistants with prompts relevant to your industry and brand, then analyzes the responses to surface actionable insights:

- **Visibility** — How often does your brand appear in AI-generated answers?
- **Citations** — Which of your URLs and content assets are referenced by AI models?
- **Sentiment** — Is your brand being described positively, neutrally, or negatively?
- **Accuracy** — Are AI models making factually correct claims about your brand?
- **Competitive share** — How does your AI presence compare to competitors?
- **Shopping intelligence** — Track product and merchant visibility in AI shopping results

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your organization">
    Add your brand's domains and configure the categories (product lines, topics, competitors) you want to track.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run prompts">
    Profound automatically runs thousands of prompts across AI bots — or you can trigger custom prompt sets via the API.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Analyze reports">
    Pull structured reports on visibility, citations, sentiment, and more using the REST API or an SDK.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Optimize content">
    Use content optimization recommendations to improve how AI models represent your brand.
  </Step>
</Steps>
